1. **Tap the + button** at the bottom right
2. **Fill in the details:**

3. **Tap "Add Item"** - Your item appears in the list!

## â“ Common Questions

**Q: Does MilkPlease need internet?**
A: No! Everything works offline.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes! Everything stays on your device.

**Q: Can I sync between devices?**
A: Not currently, but that's a planned feature!

**Q: Can multiple people edit the same list?**
A: Not yet, but it's coming soon!

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: On your phone's local storage. It survives app updates and reinstalls.

**Q: How do I backup my lists?**
A: Generate PDF files and save them to your computer or cloud storage.

**Q: Can I delete everything?**
A: Currently, items are deleted individually. A "clear all" option is coming soon.
